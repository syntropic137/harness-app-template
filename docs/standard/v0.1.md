## Mental model (one-paragraph extract)

To give the reader something while the port is queued: the harness is a **tool belt with named slots**; each loop on the belt is a contract; each tool that fits is a plugin; a **template** is one specific assembly of plugins. The contracts are stable (the Standard) and the plugins are swappable. Read [`docs/harness-engineering/README.md`](../harness-engineering/README.md) for the canonical references catalog and the five load-bearing principles.
