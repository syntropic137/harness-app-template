## v0.4.0 — Gap-closure: from extracted shape to enforced governance

**Context.** The initial extraction carried the *shape* of the harness (slot
picks, ADRs, plumbing) but dropped the *discipline + governance* layer that the
upstream lab had accumulated. A five-pass, evidence-based gap analysis
(`docs/gap-analysis/00-consolidated.md`) inventoried what was missing and drove
the closure under epic `create-harness-app-n48`.

**What changed.**

- **Architectural governance is now enforced on every run.** The `sensors` slot
  graduated from report-only to an **enforcing pre-push gate** in
  baseline-snapshot mode (fail-on-worsening) — the core "governance every run"
  goal, in the evolutionary-architecture / fitness-function sense.
- **Full measurement set.** Sensor adapters now cover Martin coupling
  (Ca/Ce/Instability/Abstractness/Distance via dependency-cruiser + ts-morph),
  **cognitive + cyclomatic complexity**, and the **APSS `apss_topology`**
  adapter — all feeding one aggregator. Additive and preservation-first
  (`ADR-0017`): APSS is canonical, sentrux + the per-language adapters are kept
  available, nothing retired.
- **Engines recovered from the lab.** `doc-validator` (Rust), `stack`
  (Node/TS), and `versioning` ported over their stubs.
- **A startup-time fitness function** (hyperfine + baseline) joins the gate set.
- **Discipline + lineage restored.** The harness-engineering canon (README,
  capability-checklist, key-passages, five principles, and the full References
  table) was ported from `neural-hermes-data`; decisions migrated to the APSS
  ADR standard under `docs/adrs/`; the upstream-update-flow + provenance check
  landed.

**Why it matters.** Forking this template now gives you a harness that *enforces*
architectural constraints automatically, not one that merely documents them.

**Pointers.** `docs/adrs/` (decisions), `docs/harness-engineering/` (discipline),
`docs/gap-analysis/` (the closure evidence), `harness/sensors/` (the gate + adapters).
